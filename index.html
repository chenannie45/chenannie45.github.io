Short Bio

I am a PhD candidate of Computer Science at Arizona State Univeristy under the supervision of Professor Hanghang Tong. I received my M.Sc degree from New York University in 2013 and B.Eng degree from Beihang University in 2011, both majored in Computer Science.

My research interest is in large scale data mining on graphs and real-world network analysis.

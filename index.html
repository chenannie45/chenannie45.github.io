Short Bio

I am an Assistant Professor at University of Virginia, Department of Computer Science. Before that, I was a Research Assistant Professor at Biocomplexity Institute, and a software engineer at Google. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science at Arizona State University under the supervision of Professor Hanghang Tong in 2019, my M.Sc degree from New York University in 2013 and B.Eng degree from Beihang University in 2011, both majored in Computer Science.
